Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some quick answers about how EmbSys Intelligence brings smart, visual automation to your home.

What is EmbSys Intelligence?

EmbSys Intelligence is a microcontroller-based system that uses visual inspection and agentic process automation to detect and respond to objects, designed specifically for home-level applications.

What kind of tasks can it perform at home?

It can recognize objects, monitor specific activities, automate responses (like turning on lights or triggering alerts), and integrate with smart home devices.

How does the visual inspection work?

It uses a built-in or external camera and processes images in real-time to identify objects or events using lightweight AI models.
