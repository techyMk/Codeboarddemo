CodeBoard at a glance: An AI analyses the images captured by the electro-optical system and identifies defects and anomalies.

Highly affordable and immediate to deploy industrial visual inspection

The CodeBoard system combines a unique electro-optical system with innovative AI technology to provide a visual quality inspection solution that is versatile, extremely easy to use, adapts to changes in the production line, and requires no machine vision expertise. Only 20 to 30 good samples are required. Bad samples are optional, not mandatory. The system can be installed on the production line, either end-of-line or mid-line, and fully integrated with PLC or MES/ERP systems. This makes it great for the inspection of incoming goods or the final quality inspection of finished products.
